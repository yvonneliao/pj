Grilled Chicken and Salmon Poke Bowl

  PREP 12 MINS         TIME 8 MINS

Bursting flavor in every bite

A symphony of both texture and taste. Artfully drizzled with a duo of sauces—a traditional soy sauce for umami depth, and a spicy sriracha mayo that provides a creamy heat, creating a flavorful kick with each bite.

  Diet: Low Calorie         Yield: 2 servings

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, and garlic powder in a medium bowl. Add the chicken pieces and toss to coat.
  2. Sauté the chicken in a skillet over medium heat for 5-7 minutes.
  1. Divide the rice and the mixed greens among two bowls.
  2. Top each bowl with grilled chicken, raw salmon, avocado, edamame, and tofu.
  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise and sriracha. Drizzle over the poke bowls.

    2. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and green onions. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Buttery Rich

  Tips

For a spicier flavor, try to swap in spicy mayo or add more sriracha sauce to the mayonnaise mixture.

You can also add other toppings such as finely chopped cilantro, fish roe, seaweed strip, or fried onions.

This recipe is great for meal prep! Make the bowls ahead of time and store them in the fridge for up to 3 days.
